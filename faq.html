Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI-powered interview system

What is the InterviewAI system?
InterviewAI is an advanced artificial intelligence platform designed to conduct realistic mock interviews, generate relevant questions, and provide detailed feedback to help candidates prepare for job interviews.
Who can benefit from using InterviewAI?
InterviewAI is beneficial for job seekers at all levels, career changers, students preparing for internships, and even hiring managers looking to improve their interview techniques.
How accurate is the AI in generating relevant questions?
Our AI achieves 92% relevance based on user feedback, with questions tailored to specific job roles, industries, and experience levels using data from thousands of real interviews.
Is my data secure when using InterviewAI?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and comply with global data protection regulations. Your interview data is never shared with third parties.
How do I get started with InterviewAI?
Simply create an account, select your target job role and experience level, and you can start practicing immediately with our AI interviewer.
What interview types does InterviewAI support?
Our system supports various interview formats including technical, behavioral, case study, and panel interviews across multiple industries and job levels.
Does the system provide feedback on my answers?
Yes, InterviewAI provides comprehensive feedback including content analysis, speech patterns, pacing, and suggestions for improvement after each mock interview.
Can I customize the interview questions?
Absolutely. You can customize by job title, company, specific skills, or even upload a job description for tailored questions.
Is there a video recording feature?
Yes, all practice interviews are recorded so you can review your performance, with AI analysis of your body language and facial expressions available in premium plans.
Does InterviewAI support multiple languages?
Currently we support English, Spanish, French, and German, with more languages coming soon.
What browsers are supported?
InterviewAI works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge with JavaScript enabled.
What are the system requirements?
You need a modern computer with a webcam and microphone. We recommend at least 4GB RAM and a stable internet connection (5Mbps minimum).
Does it work on mobile devices?
Yes, our responsive design works on smartphones and tablets, though we recommend larger screens for the best experience.
How is the AI trained?
Our AI models are trained on thousands of real interview transcripts and continuously improved with user feedback while maintaining strict privacy standards.
What if I experience technical issues?
Our support team is available 24/7 via chat and email, with an average response time of under 2 hours for technical issues.
