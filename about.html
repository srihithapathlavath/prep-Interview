About Our System

Learn how our AI-powered interview question generation system works and the technology behind it.

Our Mission

At InterviewAI, we're on a mission to transform the interview process for both job seekers and hiring teams. We believe that interviews should be fair, efficient, and focused on finding the right match between candidates and positions.

Our AI-powered system helps job seekers prepare more effectively while enabling HR professionals to create consistent, comprehensive interview processes that identify the best talent.

How It Works

1

Upload Documents

Upload your resume. Our system supports multiple file formats including PDF, DOCX, and plain text.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI analyzes the documents to identify key skills, requirements, and job responsibilities, extracting the most relevant information.

3

Question Generation

Based on the analysis, the system generates customized interview questions tailored to the specific job and candidate profile.
